About Our Company


Our main services are concentrated in the following fields: commercial fuel cells, ultraconductors, distributed tidal energy system. And we are now expanding our research and service in bomass & biofuels cultivation, fermentation process and alkaline land remediation.

Besides the commerical products like personal scaled solar panels and ultraconductor based batteries which are already commonly used by billions of users, so far we have already been responsible for two national renewable energy projects: Solar Power Generator on Taklimakan Desert in South Xinjiang and Tidal Energy Power Station of Tai-O in Hong Kong. And our recent plan is going to remediate the alkaline soil on which we will cultivate biomass and biofuel oriented crops in Gansu. We are also looking for qualified experts and new grads who are specilized in science and engineering fields (mainly in Electricial Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil and Hydraulic Engineering, Computer Science, Material Science, Chemical Engineering and Biotechnology) to join our team. Interested? Please contact us!


Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to making money by protecting the environment and this world. For us, environmtnal friendness and development of technology are of the same importance. We hope that with our technology, both human beings and the nature will grow in a healthy and harmonious way.


VISION: Our vision expands further than any specialized technology field, because we know environmental engineering and technology is a comprehensive field and is (or should be) composed of different technicals. The traditional environmental technologies made benefit mainly from government's financial aid and NGO's support. We believe that a field will never be really developed until it is profitable. So our research emphasis has been and will always be concentrated on those profitable or potentially profitable technologies.
